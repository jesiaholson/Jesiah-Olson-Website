How to Create a Social Media Page

Steps to Get Started

  1. Picking a Platform

    • FaceBook
    • Twitter
    • YouTube
    • Instagram
    • TikTok
    • Or any other platform

  2. Making Your First Post

    • Use a high quality photo/video
    • Include a well thought description about your photo/video
    • Add hashtags relevent to your content (i.e. Tree photo = #nature)
    • Include a location (optional)
    • Double-check all features for typos, bad hashtags, etc...)

  3. Sharing Your Post

    • Copy link or directly share post to platform story
    • Continue to add new hastags to reach a broader audience
    • Get other channels to re-post your photo
    • Ask friends to like, share, and comment
    • If you created multiple platforms, share on those too!
